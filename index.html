Privacy

Your journal stays off our servers.

Tendi stores your mood entries, notes, photos, voice notes, and insights on your device by default. If you enable iCloud sync, your data syncs through your personal Apple iCloud account, not a Tendi journal server.

FAQ

A few practical answers.

Is my data private?

Yes. Journal content is stored on your device by default. If you enable iCloud sync, it syncs through your personal Apple iCloud account, not a Tendi journal server.

How much does Tendi cost?

Tendi will be free at launch.

When does it launch?

Tendi is planned for summer 2026. Join the list and we will email when it is ready.

Which devices are supported?

Tendi supports iPhone on iOS 17.2 or later, with an Apple Watch companion app for watchOS 10 or later so you can make quick check-ins from your wrist.

Do I need an account?

No. Tendi is built to work without an account.
